About Us

We are world's largest professional experts since registration in the year 2020. We offer financial and business advisory services to diverse Kenyan and international organizations. With our skilled and experienced partners, we provide a comprehensive range of business solutions in accounting, tax advisory, payroll management, auditing services etc.

Whether you are an individual or corporate client requiring outsourced accountants, need help in starting a new business, setting up your accounting and control systems, handling your payroll or getting assurance services, Nedvel Consultants is your friendly expert.
